   THANK GOD I'M A COUNTRY BOY  JOHN DENVER

WELL LIFE ON A FARM IS KIND A LAID BACK
AINT MUCH AN OLD COUNTRY BOY LIKE ME CAN'T HACK
IT'S EARLY TO RISE EARLY IN THE SACK
THANK GOD I'M A COUNTRY BOY

A SIMPLE KIND A LIFE  NEVER DID ME NO HARM    
RAISIN ME A FAMILY AND WORKIN' ON A FARM
MY DAY'S ARE FILLED WITH AN AESY COUNTRY CHARM
THANK GOD I'M A COUNTRY BOY

    WELL I GOT ME  A FINE WIFE I GOT ME OLD FIDDLE
    WHEN THE SUN'S COMIN' UP I GOT CAKES ON THE GRIDDLE
    LIFE AIN'T NOTHING BUT A FUNNY FUNNY RIDDLE
    THANK GOD I'M A COUNTRY BOY

WHEN THE WORK ALL DONE AND THE SUN 'S SETTIN' LOW
I PULL OUT MY FIDDLE AND I ROSIN UP THE BOW
THE KINDS ARE A SLEEP SO I KEEP IT KIND A LOW
THANK GOD I'M A COUNTRY BOY

I'D PLAY SALLY GOODIN ALL DAY IF I COULD
BUT THE LORD AND MY WIFE WOULDN'T TAKE IT VERY GOOD
SO I FIDDLE WHEN I CAN AND I WORK WHEN I SHOULD
THANK GOD I'M A COUNTRYBOY

    REFR.

I WOULDN'T TRADE ME FOR DAIMONDS OR JEWELS

THANK GOD I'M A COUNTRY BOY

YEAH CITY FOLK DRIVIN' IN A BLACK LIMOUSINE

THANK GOD I'M A COUNTRY BOY

    REFR.

THANK GOD I'M A COUNTRY BOY
