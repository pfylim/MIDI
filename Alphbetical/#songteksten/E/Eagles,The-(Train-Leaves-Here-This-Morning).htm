This song is another country addition by Leadon off the

first album.  I was planning on figuring it out later

this week, but I sat down with it today and got it in

less than 10 minutes.  I was impressed.  Well, enough

about me, here's the song.  Have fun!




This is the intro.  I decided to tab it out so it would be

easier to read and figure out, because Bernie plays with the

chords a little.  What I have here keeps repeating whenever

I say E in my tab.  There's also a swing feel on this, so

the beats aren't all equal.  Play it and you'll see what I mean.

It starts the song, and continues up to the lyrics below.
