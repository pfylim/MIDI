THE LAST RESORT

Don Henley & Glenn Frey



Eagles

Hotel California

1976



This is one of my all-time favorite Eagles songs.  I love to play it on the

piano, but since I can't very well write out the piano music I'll do guitar

instead.  Have fun with it and e-mail me with any questions you may have.



The piano plays lots of this song, so if you see a chord in parenthesis,

like (E), it means the acoustic does not play there.  When the chord appears normally,

like E, that means the acoustic is playing.  [If you see C(addD), that's a chord name,

not something in parenthesis].
