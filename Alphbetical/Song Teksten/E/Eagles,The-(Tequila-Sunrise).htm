Eagles

Desperado

The acoustic part was originally put out by Mike Mongeau, but I modified it slightly.

It's my favorite Eagles song to play, and I hope you have fun with it too.  You'll now

find an electric part at the end.  I hope you can follow it.  It's from the Hell Freezes

Over version, so don't try to follow it from the album version.  Have a blast, and enjoy!
