LOVE WILL KEEP US ALIVE

Pete Vale, Jim Capaldi & Paul Carrack



Eagles

Hell Freezes Over

1994





This is another of their new songs, a really good one off Hell Freezes Over.  It's

really really simple to play because there are 3 easy guitar parts plus an important bass

part (which is what you get for letting your bassist sing).  Here we go.  I know lots

of you have asked for this song, so you'd better have fun with it :-).





  GUITAR 1 plays the main riff you hear throughout the song.

      It only changes during the bridge, and I'll indicate the changes then;

      otherwise it just does the thing shown below (about 18 times).

  GUITAR 2 is an electric that arpeggiates (slow strum through the chord) the chords shown

      above the riff (only on the first beat of every chord change).

      It plays the first time this riff is played, then it stops.

      When it plays later, it is indicated by the chord over the lyrics.

  GUITAR 3 plays the solos and some sliding chords during the bridge.

      It is in open D tuning: (low to high) D A D F# A D and always uses a slide.
